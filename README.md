# AIPM Bootcamp – AI-Powered User Research

!!! Do not make this repository public!!!

## 🎯 Goal
Validate your Day 3 prototype through **AI-assisted user research**.  
You’ll simulate interviews, analyze synthetic feedback, design a real-user plan, and define success metrics.

By the end of this module, you’ll have a **research-ready plan** to test your prototype with real users.

---

## 📋 Framework Overview
**Total Time:** ~65 minutes + team sharing

- **Step 1:** Synthetic Interviews (15 min)  
- **Step 2:** Pattern Analysis (10 min)  
- **Step 3:** Real User Planning (10 min)  
- **Step 4:** Data Strategy Design (15 min)  
- **Step 5:** Build a Permanent AI Research Assistant (15 min)

Final: Team sharing & integration planning (15 min)

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Start with `00-synthetic-interviews.md` to simulate interviews with your personas.  
2. Follow each step sequentially to analyze, plan, and operationalize your research.  
3. End with `04-research-assistant.md` to create a custom GPT that automates future research.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Needed
- ChatGPT (GPT-4 or GPT-5) or Claude for AI interviews  
- Your personas (from Day 2 Discovery)  
- Prototype link or screenshots (from Day 3 Prototyping)  
- ~75 minutes of focused work  

---

## 🧠 Learning Objectives
By completing this module, you will:
- Run AI-based synthetic interviews and interpret their limits  
- Extract patterns and spot AI biases  
- Plan validation with real users  
- Design a data strategy to measure user value  
- Build a custom AI research assistant for continuous learning
